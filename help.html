Kanban

What is Kanban and what is it used for?

Kanban is a system for agile project management. It originally comes from Japan - more precisely from the Toyota Motor Corporation. The system became particularly well-known through Microsoft, who adapted the Kanban principle. Kanban means something like signal sign.

How do I use the board?

  • On the board each task passes through 4 stages.

  • The tasks can simply be moved by drag and drop to the needed section or be deleted if not needed anymore.

  • The color of the left border of each task illustrates its importance level.

  • By click on the task you can see all details.

How do I use the Add Task?

  • Create new tasks in the add-task section.

  • Fill the form and choose by whom the task should be processed.

  • You can create new categories for your individual purpose.

How do I use the backlog?

  • The backlog is a collection of upcoming tasks and ideas that are not yet in the focus of the project team.

  • To move the task to the board click on the plus icon.

  • If task not needed it can be deleted by the trash icon.

  • By click on the task you can see more details.
